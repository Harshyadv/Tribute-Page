Shin Megami Tensei

The Pillars of a Cult Classic

  • Engaging Storytelling: Deep narratives exploring morality, choice, and existentialism.
  • Unique Game Mechanics: Demon negotiation and recruitment add strategic depth.
  • Press Turn System: A punishing yet rewarding combat system that defined a genre.
  • Iconic Art Direction: The "Demon Artist" Kazuma Kaneko’s cold, porcelain-like character designs.
  • Urban Apocalypse: Familiar modern Tokyo twisted into a mythic, desolate ruin.
  • Demon Fusion: The occult science of combining allies to create forbidden power.
